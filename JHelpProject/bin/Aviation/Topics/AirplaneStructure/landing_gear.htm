Landing Gear

The landing gear's function seems obvious, but it actually serves the following two purposes:

  • Absorb the landing load when the airplane lands.
  • Support the airplane as it taxis on the ground.

Landing gear is either retractable (retracts into the airplane during flight) or fixed (stays extended during flight).
