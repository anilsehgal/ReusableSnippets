Runway Markings

Runway markings are different depending on whether the runway is equipped to handle IFR flights (flights in which the pilot relies on instruments to fly and land the plane) or VFR flights (flights that do not require instruments).

Runway markings provide important information such as the runway heading and distance markings. A sharp pilot can tell how far down the runway he or she is by paying attention to the distance markings passed during a landing.
